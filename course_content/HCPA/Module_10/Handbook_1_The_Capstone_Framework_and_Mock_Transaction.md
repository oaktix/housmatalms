# MODULE 10: Capstone & Certification

## Handbook 1: The Capstone Framework & Mock Transaction

*"Theory teaches you the rules; the capstone proves you can play the game."*

### Opening Story
Consider two pilot trainees. 

The first trainee reads every flight manual, scores 100% on all written tests, and can explain the aerodynamics of a jet engine perfectly. However, he has never sat inside a cockpit or operated a flight simulator under crosswind conditions.

The second trainee completes the same manuals, but also logs 100 hours in a flight simulator, facing simulated engine failures, heavy storms, and landing gear jams. 

When a real storm hits a passenger flight, who would you trust to fly the plane?

The Capstone Project is your flight simulator. We do not certify Property Advisors based on their ability to memorize textbooks. We certify them when they demonstrate they can navigate a complex property transaction, solve real-world problems, and protect their clients under operational pressure.

### Lesson 1: The Capstone Simulation Scope

The **Capstone Project** is an integrated simulation of a complete property transaction. You will be assigned a mock buyer profile and a mock property portfolio. You must execute all steps of the transaction using the Housmata principles and platform:

#### The 5 Phases of the Capstone:
1. **Client Profiling & Consultation:** Conduct a mock consultation with a client. Uncover their goals, timeline, DTI limits, and risk profile. Document this in the CRM.
2. **Property Search & Verification:** Select a property from the mock portfolio that matches the client's profile. Pick its coordinates, run a registry search, and draft a **Due Diligence & Risk Report**.
3. **Financial Structuring:** Prepare an affordability sheet. Calculate the total Cash-to-Close and monthly repayment schedule (if using a mortgage).
4. **Contract & Closing:** Generate the digital Offer Letter and Contract of Sale. Guide the client to sign using secure digital signatures. Issue the digital payment receipt.
5. **After-Sales Plan:** Prepare a 1-year property management or investor reporting schedule for the asset.

---

### Lesson 2: The Mock Transaction Portfolio

For the simulation, you will receive:
- **The Buyer Profile Sheet:** Detailing the buyer's income, savings, location preference, and family needs.
- **The Property Documents Folder:** Containing copies of survey plans, deeds, and corporate CAC papers (some of which contain intentional defects and red flags).
- **The Platform Sandbox:** A mock instance of the Housmata Advisor app where you will log logs, upload files, and generate contracts.

---

### Lesson 3: The Grading Rubric

Your submission will be graded by the Academy Examination Board based on these five criteria:

| Evaluation Area | Weight | Target Standard |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Due Diligence Depth** | 30% | Did you identify all hidden document defects, setback violations, and coordinate errors? |
| **Financial Accuracy** | 20% | Are your DTI calculations, CAGR estimates, and Cash-to-Close tallies 100% correct? |
| **CRM Discipline** | 20% | Are your communication logs professional, narrative, and detailed? |
| **Legal Compliance** | 15% | Are the digital contracts correctly filled, and are all necessary signatures present? |
| **Professional Presentation** | 15% | Is the final Transaction Binder structured, clean, and easy for the client to read? |

*Note:* A score of **75% or higher** in each area is required to pass. If you fail any section, you must re-submit that phase of the project.

### Capstone Check-off List
Ensure your final binder includes the following files:

- `[ ]` **Client Consultation Log:** PDF printout of the CRM profile and meeting notes.
- `[ ]` **Verification coordinates search report:** Showing coordinate search results and zoning checks.
- `[ ]` **Housmata Due Diligence & Risk Report:** The completed 4-Pillars audit.
- `[ ]` **Affordability sheet:** Detailed cash-to-close and DTI calculations.
- `[ ]` **Signed digital Contract of Sale:** Legal agreement with e-signature certificates.
- `[ ]` **Transaction summary cover letter:** A premium letter to the client summarizing the deal.

### Chapter Summary
- The Capstone Simulation is a practical, end-to-end test of your advisory skills.
- The project covers five phases: Profiling, Verification, Financials, Closing, and After-Sales.
- Evaluation is based on diligence, accuracy, logging discipline, compliance, and presentation.
- A passing grade of 75% across all rubrics is mandatory for certification.
